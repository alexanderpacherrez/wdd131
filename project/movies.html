FAVORITE MOVIES

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

2016 . PG-13 . 2h 13m

Was it just to fill a movie script hole? Rogue One really cannot be considered this way, if I love something about Star Wars it is that there is not really a good or bad ending, although in this case there is, for people who knew what their life was worth it was, it simply was. It hurt to see them die, but at the same time it filled me with joy and admiration. I have no doubt that the Darth Vader scene is one of the best in recent years, its essence is shown here.

If you ask me what my favorite Star Wars movie is, without a doubt it is this one.

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe

2005 . PG . 2h 23m

From this movie I learned that things exist far beyond our imagination. The good will always be good and the bad will always be bad. I love the final part when the Pevensies brothers want to tell the professor what happened but they think that maybe he wouldn't believe them, he looks at them and says "Try me."

X-Men: Days of Future Past

2014 . PG-13 . 2h 12m

"Just because someone stumbles and gets lost doesn't mean they are lost forever." Every time I read this phrase it gives me the courage to continue in the face of all kinds of situations. If this movie taught me anything, it is that our future is not defined, we are responsible for what we want to achieve.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2

2017 . PG-13 . 2h 16m

The incredible music of this movie captivates me every time I have the opportunity to see it, the performances of each of the characters really make me feel close to them, I especially love getting to know more about Star Lord and Yondu, their father to son connection simply It's magic. One of the phrases I love the most is "I don't use my head to fly the arrow, boy! I use my heart."

The Butterfly Effect

2004 . R . 1h 53m

If you could travel to the past, what would you change? The premise of this film means that anyone who sees it can quickly become involved in what is happening to Evan Treborn's character. I could say that this was one of the films that made my imagination fly, sometimes I have thought that by reading my diaries I can also do the same, unfortunately that is not the case.
