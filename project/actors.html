FAVORITE ACTORS

Leonardo DiCaprio

Producer . Actor . Writer

It has been said many times that he who perseveres achieves. In this case it was, Leo is one of the best actors I have had the blessing of seeing act, the various roles that he has been able to play are a simple example of this. I felt a lot of joy when in 2016 he managed to win the Oscar for best actor, he really deserves it, his performances and his dedication to the world of acting stand out.
